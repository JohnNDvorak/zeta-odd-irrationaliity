# Dupont 2018 notes

- Dupont-style parity constraints are useful, but parity alone is not a support theorem.
- For affine families `s_i(n) = u_i n + v_i`, the all-`n` parity constraints used here are:
  - `u_i - u_j in Z` for all `i, j`
  - `v_i - v_j in Z` for all `i, j`
- The engine uses parity as a structural filter, not as a certificate of exact weight support.
