# Brown 2014 / 2016 notes

- Treat Brown's `M_0,8` census as fixed ground truth for this engine.
- For `N = 8`, there are `17` convergent configurations and `13` distinct linear-form families.
- `8pi8` is the unique odd family with support in `{1, zeta(3), zeta(5)}`.
- `8pi8v` is the dual family with natural support in `{1, zeta(2), 2 zeta(5) + 4 zeta(3) zeta(2)}`.
- The repo should preserve the distinction between configuration count and family count.
